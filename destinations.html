Destinations

Every Corner
of Bohol

From the iconic to the undiscovered — the places that define this island province.

Iconic

The Icons of Bohol

Nature

Chocolate Hills

1,268 symmetrical hills across three municipalities. Brown in dry season, green in wet — a UNESCO World Heritage nominee and the symbol of Bohol.

Beach

Panglao Island

Alona Beach, Dumaluan Beach, world-class dive sites, and Balicasag's marine sanctuary — Panglao is Bohol's undisputed beach capital.

Experience

Loboc River

The Loboc River loops for 30 km through jungle, villages, and ancient bamboo groves. Cruise it aboard a floating restaurant for an experience unlike any other.

Hidden Gems 2026

Off the Beaten Path

Scenery

Cadapdapan Rice Terraces

A peaceful alternative to the more touristy rice terraces in the north. Candijay's green paddies cascade down a hillside with a waterfall as backdrop.

Waterfall

Can-umantad Falls

Bohol's tallest waterfall in Candijay — a powerful 10-metre-wide curtain of water falling into a wide emerald pool. The trek through forest is part of the reward.

Hiking

Binabaje Hills, Alicia

Known locally as "Little Switzerland," the Binabaje ridgeline offers sweeping mountain panoramas across rolling hills. A moderately challenging hike with dramatic payoffs.

Heritage

Lamanoc Island, Anda

A mystical islet in the municipality of Anda featuring pre-colonial burial caves and ancient rock art. One of the Philippines' most undervisited archaeological sites.

Night Tour

Abatan River Firefly Tour

A nighttime banca ride through mangrove forests transformed into a living galaxy of wild fireflies. One of Bohol's most magical and least crowded experiences.

Island

Virgin Island, Panglao

A tiny sandbar island accessible by boat from Alona Beach. Dazzlingly white, surrounded by shallow turquoise water — ideal for a half-day escape.

Events 2026

Bohol's Festival Calendar

January

Ubi Festival

Celebration of the ube kinampay — Bohol's prized purple yam. Features food festivals, cooking competitions, and agri-tourism showcases.

May

Fiesta Month

Town-wide celebrations across Bohol's 47 municipalities featuring street parties, traditional games, and feasts open to visitors.

July 1–27

Sandugo Festival

The flagship cultural event of Bohol. Month-long celebrations in Tagbilaran City: reenactment of the 1565 Blood Compact, street dancing competitions, the Miss Bohol Sandugo pageant, and cultural exhibits.

August

Panagat Sa Dauis Festival

A fishing festival in Dauis municipality celebrating the coastal community's way of life through boat races, seafood fairs, and cultural performances.
