Local Experiences

Feel the
Heart of Bohol

Beyond the sights lies a rich tapestry of flavors, traditions, and warmth that defines the daily life of Boholanos.

River Life

The Loboc River Experience

The Loboc River is the lifeblood of its community. A journey down its emerald waters offers a glimpse into the lush landscape and soulful music of the region.

Flavors

A Taste of Tradition

Delicacy

Peanut Kisses

Inspired by the Chocolate Hills, these signature cookies are light, crunchy, and made from high-quality peanuts and egg whites.

Sweet

Boholano Calamay

A sticky, sweet delicacy made from glutinous rice, coconut milk, and sugar, traditionally served in a smooth coconut shell.

Drink

Sikwate (Hot Chocolate)

Pure Boholano cacao tablets (tablea) melted into a rich, dark hot chocolate, usually paired with Puto Maya (sticky rice).

Heritage

Festivals & Art

Festival

Sandugo Festival

A month-long celebration every July in Tagbilaran City, commemorating the historic 1565 Blood Compact between Sikatuna and Legazpi.

Craft

Antequera Basket Weaving

The town of Antequera is known for its intricate basketry made from bamboo, rattan, and nito vines—a tradition passed down for generations.
